About Samtse Ferro Alloys Limited

Company Background

Established in 2024, Samtse Ferro Alloy Company has quickly emerged as a leading producer of high-quality ferro silicon. Strategically located in Bhutan, our company leverages the region's abundant natural resources and consistent power supply to deliver exceptional products to our global clientele. With a dedicated team and state-of-the-art facilities, we are committed to maintaining the highest standards of production and sustainability, ensuring that we meet the diverse needs of industries worldwide.

Production Capacity

Our production process is powered by two advanced furnaces, each utilizing 18 MVA of power to ensure the highest efficiency and product quality. We are committed to delivering superior ferro silicon to meet the diverse needs of our global clients, supporting various industries with our reliable and consistent supply.

Environmental Commitement

At Samtse Ferro Alloy Company, we are deeply committed to the philosophy of Gross National Happiness (GNH), which guides our approach to business and environmental stewardship. Preservation of the environment is a cornerstone of GNH, and we fully comply with all environmental policies set forth by the Royal Government of Bhutan.
